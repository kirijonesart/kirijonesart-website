I am an artist, teacher, Royal Academy Schools alumnus, and Forest School practitioner passionate about helping people reconnect with creativity through art, nature, and hands-on learning.

My practice is rooted in curiosity, observation, and making — whether in the studio, outdoors among trees and wild spaces, or alongside learners discovering their own creative voice.

## Art. Nature. Creative Learning.

Art has the power to deepen how we see the world and our place within it. My work brings together fine art, teaching, and the natural world to create thoughtful, engaging experiences for all.

As an RA Schools alumnus, I bring a strong foundation in contemporary art practice. As a teacher and Forest School practitioner, I believe creativity flourishes through exploration, experimentation, and connection with the natural world.

Whether leading workshops, developing collaborative projects, or creating artwork inspired by landscape and environment, I aim to make spaces where people feel confident to observe, imagine, and create.
